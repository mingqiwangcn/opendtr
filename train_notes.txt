1) Create training data by
    ./create_retrieval_data.sh or
    ./create_retrieval_data_syt.sh

2) Train a dual encoder by
    ./train_encoder.sh or
    ./train_encoder_syt.sh

3) Evaulate by
    ./eval_checkpoints.sh or
    ./eval_checkpoints_syt.sh

4) Encode test and table data by
    ./encode_tables.sh or
    ./encode_tables_st.sh

5) Predict by
    ./retrieve_table.sh or
    ./retrieve_table_syt.sh
